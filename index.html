About Us

TechTogether works to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized students in the interdisciplinary fields of technology. We aim to inspire our community and provide them with the confidence to pursue their curiosities.
TechTogether was originally founded in 2017 as SheHacks by a group of 13 students across Boston. What was once a one-time event has transformed into a nonprofit after the founders realized the impact gender-focused events had on gender-marginalized communities. Now, TechTogether has extended from Boston to other major cities nationwide, including Seattle!
